psycho magnet albümünün 8. şarkısı. (bkz: london after midnight)
adı ruins olarak da geçen güzel london after midnight şarkısı. 1998 çıkışlı psycho magnet albümünde bulunur. shatter (all my dead friends) ve sacrifice ile beraber en çok l.a.m. ruhu taşıyan şarkılardan biri olduğunu iddia ederim. neyime güvenerek ederim? london after midnight dinlerim, severek dinlerim. sözlerini de yazarım, tam olur. i've knelt at your alter- i've cut out my heart- i've lived in your ruins- my pain is your art- my wrists are bound tight so as not to bleed- my eyes were so dark so as not to see- the memories of the past are fading like a bad dream- i've never been so alone than with you, i've never been scared to dream until now, i can't close my eyes, i'll carry on, screaming- your words are like ice, they melt in the heat-the cold and the pain which you seem to breed- have become what you are and left your ruins empty.
insanın aklına edvard munch'un çığlığını* getiren güzel şarkı.
