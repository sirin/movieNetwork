mindscape firmasının 1988 tarihli bilgisayar oyunu. müziklerinin bir kısmı jean-michel jarre'e ait olup, www.mobygames.com adresinde oyun hakkında şunlar yazılıdır: you play a computer game programmer (captain blood) who finds himself trapped in one of his own computer games. worse yet, you've been cloned. each of your five clones has made off with a quantity of your bodily fluids and is hiding out on a planet somewhere in the galaxy. because of your depleted bodily fluids, you're gradually turning into a machine. you need to find and assimilate those clones before the transformation is complete.
klasik olarak anilan eski bir sci-fi adventure. pc, c64 ve amstrad versiyonlari vardir. oyunun en can alici ve zevkli yani diyalog sistemidir. not: pc versiyonu biraz sorunludur.
